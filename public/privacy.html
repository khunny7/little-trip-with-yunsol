Little Trip with Yunsol

Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Little Trip with Yunsol (the “App”) helps families discover toddler‑friendly places. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account info you provide (for example, email) when you sign in.
  • Content you create in the App (for example, saved places, favorites, or tips).
  • Basic device and usage data needed to run the App reliably and securely.

How we use information

  • To provide core features (authentication, saving and retrieving your content).
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • To improve reliability and performance.

Storage and processing

The App uses Google Firebase services (Authentication and Firestore) to store data securely in the cloud. Your data is processed according to Google’s terms and regional infrastructure.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information if required by law or to protect the App and its users.

Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your content.

Children’s privacy

The App is intended for use by parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For questions or requests, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page.
